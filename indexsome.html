Ultra Protector

An autonomous turret that protects your computer by shooting plastic balls at hostile targets.

Learn More

Project Description

What Is It?

Ultra Protector is an automated defense turret that mounts near your computer and shoots plastic balls at anyone who tries to access it without permission.

The Problem

Computers are vulnerable to physical intrusion. Software security can't stop someone from physically sitting down and using your machine when you're away.

Why It Matters

Physical security of personal devices is often ignored. Ultra Protector adds a first line of physical defense that acts before anyone touches the keyboard.

What Makes It Unique

Unlike alarms or software locks, Ultra Protector responds physically — shooting harmless plastic balls to surprise and deter intruders instantly.

How It Works

1

System Armed

The owner activates Ultra Protector when leaving the workstation. The turret enters active scanning mode.

2

Motion Detected

The PIR motion sensor detects movement in the area and identifies it as a potential threat.

3

Turret Aims

The servo motor rotates the barrel to face the direction of the detected target.

4

Ball Fired

A harmless plastic ball is launched toward the intruder, surprising and deterring them.

5

Reset

The system resets and continues monitoring until the owner disarms it.

Features

Motion Detection

Continuously scans using a PIR sensor.

Auto-Targeting

Servo motors rotate the barrel toward the threat.

Ball Launcher

Fires a safe plastic ball at the detected target.

Arm / Disarm

Simple button to activate or deactivate the system.

Fast Response

Reacts in under 3 seconds from detection to firing.

Desk Mountable

Fits on any desk or shelf with an adjustable bracket.
